ABOUT KMG ENVIRONMENTAL SOLUTIONS SERVICES (PTY) LTD (PTY) LTD)**

KMG Environmental Solutions Services (Pty) Ltd is a fully accredited and multidisciplinary consultancy specialising in environmental management, ESG advisory, waste and asbestos compliance, and occupational hygiene services. Operating across all nine provinces of South Africa and the broader SADC region, we deliver scientifically robust, regulationaligned, and outcomefocused solutions that support sustainable development, legal compliance, and responsible resource stewardship.

Our firm is accredited by the Department of Employment and Labour (DoEL) as an Approved Asbestos Contractor (RAC2024-CI/100), authorising us to manage asbestos-related risks through compliant assessment, handling, removal, and disposal processes. We are also a SACNASP- and EAPASA-accredited training provider, enabling us to offer professional, industry-recognised training aligned with national scientific and environmental legislation. As a registered member of the Green Building Council of South Africa (GBCSA), we actively promote sustainable built environments and climate-resilient project planning.

KMGs strength lies in our team of professionally registered and sector-recognised specialists, ensuring that all services meet high standards of scientific integrity, credibility, and legal defensibility. Our professionals are registered with:

       SACNASP South African Council for Natural Scientific Professions

       EAPASA Environmental Assessment Practitioners Association of South Africa

       WISA Water Institute of Southern Africa

       IIAV International Institute of Acoustics and Vibration

       IAIAsa International Association for Impact Assessment (SA)

       SAIOH South African Institute for Occupational Hygiene

       GBCSA Green Building Council of South Africa

       Specialist Heritage, Archaeology, Soil Science, GIS and related professional bodies

With this strong accreditation and professional foundation, KMG provides a comprehensive portfolio of environmental and sustainability servicesincluding specialist studies, monitoring, permitting, ESG reporting, auditing, waste management, asbestos services, and accredited training. Our approach integrates scientific excellence with practical compliance solutions to support clients in the mining, infrastructure, water, industrial, municipal, renewable energy, and land development sectors.

KMG remains committed to delivering highquality, evidencebased environmental solutions that protect ecosystems, support community wellbeing, and enable organisations to achieve their environmental, governance, and sustainability objectives. Our growing national presence and accredited team position us as a trusted, ethical, and forwardthinking partner in South Africas environmental and sustainability landscape.

OUR APPROACH / METHODOLOGY

KMG follows a structured, evidence-based, and regulatory-aligned methodology across all services:

1. Baseline Understanding

We begin with thorough data collection, desktop assessments, field surveys, and stakeholder insights to establish existing environmental and social conditions.

2. Impact Identification & Assessment

Using national protocols, scientific tools, modelling techniques, and risk frameworks, we identify potential impacts and evaluate their significance.

3. Compliance Integration

We align projects with applicable legislation, guidelines, licence conditions, and industry standards.

4. Mitigation & Sustainability Measures

We provide realistic, cost-effective mitigation, rehabilitation, and optimisation strategies informed by best practice.

5. Implementation Support

Our team assists with monitoring programmes, audits, training, and compliance management to support ongoing project success.

6. Continuous Improvement

We promote adaptive management, performance tracking, and knowledge transfer to enhance long-term outcomes.
