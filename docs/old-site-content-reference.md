## Asbestos Services (services-asbestos-waste-handling)

KMG Environmental Solutions Services is a fully certified asbestos contractor, approved by the Department of Labour with certificate number **RAC2024-CI/100**. Our services ensure the safe and compliant management of asbestos-containing materials (ACMs), following stringent South African regulations to protect human health and the environment.

### 1. Asbestos Identification and Survey
- Comprehensive surveys to identify asbestos in buildings, structures, and other environments.
- Detailed assessments to determine the type, condition, and extent of asbestos materials.

### 2. Risk Assessment and Management
- Conducting thorough risk assessments to evaluate potential hazards.
- Development of asbestos management plans tailored to site-specific needs.

### 3. Asbestos Removal
- Safe removal of asbestos materials using approved techniques to minimize exposure and contamination.
- Decontamination of areas to ensure they are asbestos-free post-removal.

### 4. Asbestos Transportation
- Licensed transportation of asbestos waste to approved disposal facilities.
- Use of specialized containment methods to prevent asbestos fiber release during transport.

### 5. Asbestos Waste Disposal
- Disposal at licensed hazardous waste facilities in full compliance with environmental regulations.
- Documentation and record-keeping to provide proof of compliant asbestos disposal.

### 6. Regulatory Compliance and Reporting
- Ensuring compliance with all South African regulations related to asbestos handling, removal, transportation, and disposal.
- Preparation and submission of required documentation, including waste manifest forms and disposal certificates.

### 7. Asbestos Awareness and Training
- Training programs to educate clients on the dangers of asbestos and safe handling practices.
- Ongoing support to ensure all stakeholders are informed and compliant.

## Training Courses

### Course 1: Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) Training

**Accreditation:** CPD-Accredited by EAPASA | 2 CPD Points (Category 2)

**Learning Outcomes:**
By the end of the training, participants will be able to:

1. **Understand Environmental Legislation**
   - Interpret and apply the National Environmental Management Act (NEMA) and related EIA regulations.
   - Identify the roles of competent authorities in the EIA process.

2. **Apply the EIA Process**
   - Conduct screening, scoping, impact identification, and significance assessment.
   - Prepare Basic Assessment Reports (BARs) and Environmental Impact Reports (EIRs).

3. **Integrate Specialist Studies into EIAs**
   - Understand when to commission biodiversity, hydrology, air quality, heritage, and other studies.
   - Incorporate specialist findings into impact ratings and mitigation strategies.

4. **Manage Stakeholder Engagement**
   - Facilitate public participation processes (PPP).
   - Capture and integrate stakeholder inputs into EIA documentation.

5. **Develop and Implement EMPs**
   - Design Environmental Management Plans that align with project phases.
   - Include monitoring, auditing, and compliance strategies.

6. **Ensure Regulatory Compliance**
   - Understand environmental auditing and post-approval enforcement processes.
   - Align EIAs with sustainable development and risk mitigation goals.

**Target Group:**
- Environmental Consultants and Practitioners
- Junior and Mid-Level EAPs (Environmental Assessment Practitioners)
- Environmental Graduates & Interns
- Municipal Officials and Government Staff
- Engineers, Developers, and Project Managers
