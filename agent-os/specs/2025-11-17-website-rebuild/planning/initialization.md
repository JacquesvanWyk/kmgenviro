## Feature Description

Rebuild the KMG Environmental Solutions website from WordPress to Laravel 12 with Filament CMS. This is a complete rebuild giving the client full content management control.

### Core Features to Build

1. **Service Management System**
   - 10 service categories with sub-services
   - Hierarchical service structure
   - Rich content for each service

2. **Team Member Profiles**
   - Staff directory with photos and bios
   - Role-based organization
   - Team member management

3. **Training Booking System**
   - Replace existing Tutor LMS
   - Course catalogue
   - Booking and enrollment management

4. **Equipment Rental System**
   - Equipment catalogue
   - Quote request functionality
   - Availability management

5. **Project Portfolio**
   - Projects organized by sector
   - Case studies and testimonials
   - Project galleries

6. **Content Management**
   - Blog/news section
   - Downloadable resources with lead capture
   - Client logos and accreditations showcase

7. **Contact & Quotes**
   - Contact forms
   - Quote request system
   - Lead management
